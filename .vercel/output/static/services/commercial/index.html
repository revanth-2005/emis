Financial Incentives You Can Claim

Accelerated Depreciation (Section 32)

40% depreciation on the solar asset in Year 1, significantly reducing your tax liability. Effective system cost drops by ~15–20% for profitable entities.

Net-Metering

Export surplus generation to the DISCOM grid and receive credits against your future bills. Policy varies by state — we identify the best option for your location.

Priority Sector Lending

Solar projects can qualify for priority-sector lending rates from banks and NBFCs, reducing financing costs.

MNRE Capital Subsidy

Certain commercial categories (educational institutions, hospitals) may qualify for central or state capital subsidies — we assess eligibility and file on your behalf.

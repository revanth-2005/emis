What We Deliver

Detailed Project Report (DPR)

Full techno-commercial report with energy yield simulation (PVsyst), financial model, IRR, and payback analysis.

EPC — Full Turnkey Execution

Engineering, procurement of ALMM-listed modules, inverters, and BOS; civil works; grid-tie commissioning.

Open Access & Captive Advisory

State-specific open access routing, banking regulations, and cross-subsidy surcharge optimisation.

RESCO & BOOT Structuring

Zero-capex models where we fund, own, and operate the plant — you pay only for consumed units at a fixed tariff.

Regulatory & DISCOM Compliance

State commission approvals, net-metering/gross-metering selection, synchronisation reports, and metering.

O&M & Remote Monitoring

SCADA-enabled monitoring, preventive maintenance schedules, PR (Performance Ratio) tracking, and annual reporting.

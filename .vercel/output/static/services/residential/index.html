Frequently Asked Questions

How much subsidy can I get under PM Surya Ghar Yojana?

Under PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana, the central government subsidy is ₹30,000/kWp for systems up to 2 kW (max ₹60,000) and ₹18,000/kWp for the 2–3 kW slab (total up to ₹78,000 for a 3 kWp system). Additional state subsidies may apply. We handle the entire portal registration and claim process.

Will my electricity bill really go to zero?

For households with adequate roof space and a monthly bill of ₹2,000–₹5,000, a well-sized grid-tied system can offset 80–100% of daytime consumption. Net-metering credits surplus generation, further reducing bills. Battery storage is optional for backup during outages.

How long does installation take?

Typically 2–5 working days for the physical installation. DISCOM approval and net-meter installation can take 2–6 weeks depending on the state. We manage all paperwork — you don't need to visit any office.

What is the minimum roof area needed?

A 1 kWp system requires approximately 80–100 sq. ft. of shadow-free roof space. A 3 kWp system (the most popular for homes) needs around 250–300 sq. ft.

Do I need a battery?

For a grid-tied system (the most common and cost-effective choice), no battery is required. Power cuts? We offer hybrid systems with battery backup — at higher upfront cost. We'll recommend what suits your electricity reliability situation.

Our Story

Founded with a Vision

Ermis was founded with a clear vision: to bridge international markets while driving the adoption of sustainable energy solutions. Our founders bring decades of combined experience in the textile value chain and have worked extensively in international locations, giving us unique insights into global trade dynamics.

Starting with a focus on renewable energy — particularly solar power installations — we are establishing ourselves as trusted partners for both residential and industrial clients. Our commitment extends beyond installations: we provide comprehensive sourcing and trading services for products across global markets.

With offices in Coimbatore and Salem, India, we are strategically positioned to serve clients across South India while expanding our global reach. We are growing our portfolio to include technology products while staying true to our core mission of sustainability and excellence.

Founded in 2026 with a vision to transform India's renewable energy landscape and facilitate seamless international trade. We bring decades of international experience to deliver excellence from day one.

Our Mission

To empower businesses and individuals with sustainable energy solutions while facilitating seamless international trade. We strive to make renewable energy accessible and affordable while connecting global markets with reliability and integrity.

Our Vision

To become a leading force in the renewable energy sector and international trade, recognised for our commitment to sustainability, innovation, and customer satisfaction. We envision a future where clean energy powers communities and global commerce thrives on trust and efficiency.

The Process

From Inquiry to Bill Savings
— Here's How It Works

We guide you step-by-step through structural design, net-metering approvals, professional installation, and final savings activation.

Start with a Free Assessment
01

Site Visit

Our engineer visits your property to inspect structural readiness, measure solar exposure, and assess your current energy consumption.

02

Design

We prepare a detailed module layout, single-line diagrams, expected generation metrics, and a precise financial payback model.

03

Approval

We manage DISCOM grid connectivity approvals, net-metering agreements, and register you on the PM Surya Ghar national portal.

04

Installation

Our certified engineering team deploys the system with zero disruption. We commission the plant and deliver all compliance papers.

05

Savings

Activate real-time app tracking. Start saving from Month 1 while we handle scheduled O&M audits to keep output at peak levels.
